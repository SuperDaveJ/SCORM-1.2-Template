Lesson Overview

As an inspector, you will be using regulatory materials, forms, policy, and reference material when performing Airworthiness Certification functions. This lesson will help you become familiar with, and understand the importance of, using FAA guidance and other resources. It will also teach you how to access these resources. Finally it will reintroduce you to FAA Orders, Notices, and Advisory Circulars (ACs).  

Upon completion of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify FAA information systems that can be used to help you perform your Airworthiness Certification functions
  • Identify the FAA regulations relevant to Airworthiness Certification
  • Identify guidance associated with Airworthiness Certification
    • FAA Orders
    • FAA Notices
    • FAA Advisory Circulars (ACs)
