Slide 1: GNSS Limitations

The limitations of the GNSS may include:

  • Gaps in coverage
  • Degradation of signal
  • Interference from both natural and man-made sources

Slide 2: Inertial System Limitations

The limitations of the inertial systems may include:

  • Latitude
  • RNP time
  • Environmental effects on alignment

Slide 3: Multisensor Limitations

This system does not have limitations under normal operating conditions when supplemented with GNSS input.
