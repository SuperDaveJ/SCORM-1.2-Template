Primary, restricted, and limited are categories for which there are FAA issued TCs. Like standard certificated aircraft, these aircraft must conform to their appropriate TC.

Light-sport is a new category and these aircraft must conform to American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM) developed, and FAA accepted, consensus standards.

Experimental certificates are issued for a wide variety of purposes, some of which the Airworthiness Certificate can be issued for an unlimited duration, and some of which must be limited to a year or less.
