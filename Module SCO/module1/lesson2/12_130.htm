Regulatory Authority for Issuing Airworthiness Certificates

No person may operate an aircraft that is eligible for registration under Title 49 USC Sections 44101-44104 unless the aircraft:

  • Has been registered by the owner with the FAA Aircraft Registry Branch, AFS-750
  • Is carrying aboard the temporary authorization required by section 47.31(b), which is an exemption to operate without a registration document in the aircraft in U.S. airspace
  • Is an aircraft in the armed forces
