Categories of Standard Airworthiness Certificates

Standard Airworthiness Certificates are further divided into seven categories. These include:

  • Normal
  • Utility
  • Acrobatic
  • Commuter
  • Transport
  • Manned Free Balloon
  • Any other Special Class of aircraft designated by the Administrator, such as gliders
