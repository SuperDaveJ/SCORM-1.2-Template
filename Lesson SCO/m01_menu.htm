In this lesson, you will learn about the regulatory materials, forms, policy, and reference materials used when performing Airworthiness Certification functions.

This lesson will provide some clarification on the terms original and recurrent certification. It will discuss classification of Airworthiness Certificates and categories of Special Airworthiness Certificates. It will identify the guidance associated with aircraft registration, identification, and registration markings. It will identify the ways to verify an aircraft registration, and it will provide the identification requirements for aircraft and products.
