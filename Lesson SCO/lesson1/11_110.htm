Relevant Regulations

Although 14 CFR, Part 21 provides the regulatory authority for issuing Standard Airworthiness Certificates, as an inspector you must also be familiar with the requirements of the following parts that must be met at the time of the issuance of an Airworthiness Certificate:

  • 14 CFR, Part 43: Maintenance, Rebuilding, and Alteration
  • 14 CFR, Part 45: Identification and Registration Marking
  • 14 CFR, Part 47: Aircraft Registration
  • 14 CFR, Part 91: General Operating and Flight Rules
